About

We are a Minecraft Bedrock Edition 1.2 practice server. We are a play to win server, purchasable ranks give you slight benefits as well as pay for our hosting and custom plugins. We are also making a faction server, it will be slightly pay to win, in order to fund both servers. The Versai Network does not plan to profit ever, we'd just like to balance hosting costs with income, and spend the rest on making Versai an even better server for you!
